About Waweza

Waweza was born from a personal journey of self-improvement and the desire to create a tool that could help others on their path to personal growth. Our team of passionate developers and life coaches came together to build an application that combines goal-setting, habit-tracking, and mood analysis in one cohesive platform.

This project is a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, showcasing our skills in web development and our commitment to creating meaningful applications.
